## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The platform serves two primary user groups:

1. Shippers
2. Drivers / Truck Owners

A shipper can create freight requests.

A driver can browse freight requests or receive AI-recommended jobs.

The system automatically recommends the most suitable driver and vehicle for each freight request.

The platform aims to reduce:

* Empty truck trips
* Logistics costs
* Freight matching delays

while increasing:

* Driver earnings
* Delivery efficiency
* Pricing transparency

## MATCHING ENGINE

When a freight request is posted:

System performs:

1. Capacity Matching

Find vehicles capable of transporting cargo.

2. Distance Matching

Find nearest available vehicles.

3. Driver Quality Matching

Consider:

* Ratings
* Success rate
* Cancellation rate
* Previous deliveries

4. Cost Matching

Compare historical freight prices.

Generate ranking score:

MatchingScore =
CapacityScore +
DistanceScore +
RatingScore +
PriceScore

Return Top 10 recommended drivers.
