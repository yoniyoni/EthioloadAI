# PAYMENT ESCROW, LIVE TRACKING, AI NEGOTIATION, MULTILINGUAL SUPPORT, AND LOGISTICS ASSISTANT IMPLEMENTATION

You are acting as a Senior Logistics Platform Architect, Fintech Architect, AI Engineer, and Security Engineer.

Analyze the existing freight exchange platform and implement the following business logic.

---

## CRITICAL BUSINESS RULE

Users must NOT be able to complete freight agreements outside the platform.

The platform must own and control the agreement process.

The platform must own and control the payment process.

The platform must own and control delivery verification.

This is required to:

* Prevent fraud
* Prevent bypassing platform commissions
* Build trust
* Maintain transaction records
* Ensure dispute resolution

The platform must function similar to:

* Uber Freight
* Upwork Escrow
* Airbnb Payments

---

## NEW FREIGHT WORKFLOW

STEP 1

Shipper creates freight request.

Includes:

* Pickup location
* Delivery location
* Cargo type
* Weight
* Volume
* Budget
* Delivery deadline

---

STEP 2

AI analyzes freight request.

AI evaluates:

* Cargo type
* Weight
* Distance
* Historical freight prices
* Current demand
* Driver availability
* Vehicle capacity

AI generates:

* Recommended price range
* Recommended vehicle type
* Recommended drivers

---

STEP 3

System shows top recommended drivers.

Ranking based on:

40% Distance

25% Rating

20% Delivery Success Rate

15% Price Competitiveness

Generate transparent score.

Example:

Driver A
95 Match Score

Driver B
91 Match Score

Driver C
89 Match Score

---

STEP 4

Shipper selects preferred driver.

Driver receives request.

Driver may:

Accept
Reject
Counter Offer

---

STEP 5

Agreement Phase

Once both parties agree:

Create digital freight contract.

Store:

* Freight details
* Driver details
* Agreed amount
* Pickup location
* Destination
* Delivery deadline

Generate PDF.

Store permanently.

---

STEP 6

MANDATORY PAYMENT ESCROW

Before shipment begins:

Shipper must pay through platform.

Supported providers:

* Chapa API
* CBE Birr API
* Telebirr API (future)

NO shipment can start without payment.

---

ESCROW LOGIC

After payment:

Funds are NOT sent immediately to driver.

Funds remain in escrow.

Status:

PAYMENT_HELD

---

STEP 7

Shipment Starts

Driver starts trip.

Tracking activated.

Live GPS begins.

---

STEP 8

Delivery Verification

Driver marks delivery completed.

Shipper confirms.

System verifies.

Status:

DELIVERED

---

STEP 9

Release Funds

After confirmation:

Platform automatically:

Releases driver payment.

Deducts platform commission.

Records transaction.

Status:

PAYMENT_RELEASED

---

STEP 10

Ratings

Both parties rate each other.

Store:

* Rating
* Review
* Delivery quality
* Communication quality
